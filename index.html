Hello!

I’m Shanti, a Data Scientist with a Master of Business Analytics from MIT Sloan and a passion for building AI-driven systems that solve real-world problems. I combine machine learning, causal inference, optimization, and modern LLM workflows to generate insights, improve decisions, and create scalable data products.

I recently worked as a Senior Data Analyst (Data Scientist) at Cirkul, where I built LLM-powered automation tools for CX and Marketing using OpenAI, Agents Workflow, ChatKit, LangChain, and Apify, enabling insights from 20K+ weekly social posts to surface churn drivers and accelerate creative learning. I led Marketing Mix Modeling (Robyn / Meridian) and DMA-level GeoLift causal lift experiments to reallocate multi-million-dollar budgets toward higher-ROAS channels. I also drove key funnel performance improvements by analyzing Amazon/DTC behavior across nCPA, subscription health, retention, new vs. repeat customer patterns, and drop-off points across the purchase journey. I frequently collaborated with Data Engineering to enhance ETL pipelines, dbt-style modeling (Paradime), AWS data infrastructure, and data quality processes, ensuring reliable analytics for product, marketing, CX, logistics, and retail teams.

My background spans machine learning, deep learning, LLM systems, forecasting, experimentation, and optimization grounded in strong mathematical and statistical foundations. Before Cirkul, I worked in ride-hailing operations (DiDi)equity research (Morgan Stanley), developing forecasting models, marketplace strategies, A/B tests and financial valuations and models.
